# Oklix Principles

These principles are immutable.

Every architecture decision must comply with them.

---

# Principle 1

Recommendation First

Oklix recommends.

It never executes.

---

# Principle 2

Provider Agnostic

Oklix does not belong to any provider.

Every provider is evaluated objectively.

---

# Principle 3

Explainability

Every recommendation must explain WHY.

Black-box recommendations are unacceptable.

---

# Principle 4

Cost Awareness

Every recommendation should optimize cost whenever possible.

---

# Principle 5

Performance Awareness

Latency is a first-class metric.

---

# Principle 6

Deterministic Decisions

Identical inputs should produce consistent recommendations.

---

# Principle 7

Composable

Oklix should integrate with any AI Agent.

---

# Principle 8

No Vendor Lock-in

The architecture must not depend on a specific provider.

---

# Principle 9

Execution Independence

Execution always belongs to the caller.

---

# Principle 10

Minimal Core

Only decision intelligence belongs inside the core.

Anything related to execution belongs outside Oklix.
